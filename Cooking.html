About cooking as my hobby!

  • I've started cooking ever since 2021.
  • I enjoy making dishes for others. But, the taste can vary between times.
  • Fun fact: Cooking is both an art and science.
  • Even though I can cook spicy dishes. I can't eat spicy food.
  • I can only do stir frying and boiling.

How I cook my foods fast and tasty.

  1. Prepare the ingredients way before cooking.
  2. Use High Heat & Fast-Cooking Methods.
  3. Use garlic, onion, and/or ginger in oil as a flavor base.
  4. Season in Layers, before cooking when cooking and after cooking.
  5. Finish with an Acid, add a squeeze of fresh lemon or lime juice or a splash of vinegar. It makes the other flavors pop.
  6. Prioritize One-Pot/One-Pan Meals, it helps minimize clean up time.
