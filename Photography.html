About photography as my hobby!

  • I started photography as a hobby in early 2023.
  • I enjoy watching nature and taking picture of those moments.
  • Fun fact: To take good photos you must have imagination and creativity.
  • Phone used: Iqoo 12
  • Camera specs:
  • Main Camera 50 MP, f/1.7, 23mm (wide), 1/1.3", 1.2µm, multi-directional PDAF, OIS
  • Telephoto Camera 64 MP, f/2.6, 70mm (periscope telephoto), 1/2.0", PDAF, OIS, 3x optical zoom
  • Ultra Wide Camera 50 MP, f/2.0, 15mm, 119˚ (ultrawide), AF
  • Extra Features: Color spectrum sensor, Dual-LED flash, HDR, panorama

How I Take my photos

  1. Clean the phone cameras.
  2. Find good photo taking angles.
  3. Find good lighting.
  4. Balance the picture.
  5. Focus on the main point of the picture.
  6. Take the photo.
  7. Edit the photo and fix the lighting and focus
